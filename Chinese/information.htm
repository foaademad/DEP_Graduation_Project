Beijing 1,210 $ / Per Couple

(3.3k review)

Beijing, the capital of China, is a city that blends ancient history with modernity. Located in the north, it is famous for historical landmarks such as the Great Wall of China and the Forbidden City. Beijing is a major political, cultural, and educational center, hosting many universities and research institutions. The city is known for its delicious cuisine, such as Peking duck. Life in Beijing is bustling and vibrant, with many markets and shopping centers. It is also a major hub for arts and culture, with numerous theaters and museums.
