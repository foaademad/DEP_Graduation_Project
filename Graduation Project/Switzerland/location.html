Location

Switzerland, located in central Europe, is renowned for its breathtaking Alpine landscapes, pristine lakes, and charming villages. The country is bordered by Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein, making it a nexus of diverse cultures and languages. Switzerland’s small size belies its international significance, with cities like Zurich and Geneva serving as global financial and diplomatic hubs.
