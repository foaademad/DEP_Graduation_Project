Location

The Maldives, an archipelago in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious resorts. Located southwest of Sri Lanka and India, the Maldives consists of 26 atolls and over 1,000 coral islands. This tropical paradise is a top destination for honeymooners, divers, and beach lovers.
